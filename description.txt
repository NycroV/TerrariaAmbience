Terraria Ambience is a mod that adds liveliness to your little Terraria world!

You can hear all sorts of things, such as crickets, ocean waves, campfire crackles, underground rumbles, and much more! Explore around a little bit!

Hopefully you can relax a bit more with this mod!

Most Recent Update:

[NOTE] Ambience volume can be separately modified via the In-game Options or the Main Menu by holding either ADD or SUBTRACT.

[NEW FEATURE] You can replace the ambience sounds with your very own (Do note: the changes to the sounds WILL NOT apply to the 'Ambience Menu.' This will also completely all modded ambience tracks that are added.)
[NEW FEATURE] You can disable as many default ambience tracks as you want with the new config option.
[IMPROVEMENT] Sounds now are in higher detail!

[BUGFIX] Fixed rain not being properly replaceable with soundpacks.
[BUGFIX] Fixed "You have an early version!" Message appearing when it should not be. (Thanks Bacterious!)
